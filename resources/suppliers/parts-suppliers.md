## Notes & Tips

1. **Always verify fitment** - 1955 "First Series" trucks can differ from "Second Series" (1955.5 and later)
2. **Join the forums** - Best source for NAPCO-specific parts leads
3. **Save core parts** - Many suppliers offer core exchange programs
4. **Document everything** - Take photos before shipping cores
5. **Compare shipping costs** - Heavy parts may be cheaper locally
6. **Buy in groups** - Combine orders with other restorers for shipping savings
7. **Check for NOS** - New Old Stock parts are often superior quality
